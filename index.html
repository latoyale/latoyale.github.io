Missio Project

After setbacks and a missed deadline I was brought in to work the Pontifical Mission Society. The goal was to put the project back on track and launch the Missio.org with two weeks. The site successfully launched and numberous bugs were resolved. The site used PHP, JavaScript, HTML5, AJAX and CSS. I also used the Parsley validator and lightbox code on the site.

  • Client: Pontifical Society
  • Date: September 2016
  • Service: Web Development
